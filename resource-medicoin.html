The MediCoin

Digital Currency, strategy & growth

What is MediCoin (MCN)?

MediCoin (MCN) is the native cryptocurrency powering the MediCureOn platform, providing a seamless, secure, and decentralized means for transactions between patients, healthcare providers, payers, and pharmaceutical companies. It is the only accepted digital currency for all services within the MediCureOn ecosystem, offering users a way to access affordable healthcare services, pay for alternative treatments, and engage in the broader healthcare marketplace.

MediCoin’s utility is designed to expand as MediCureOn grows, offering discounted healthcare services, incentivizing healthy living, and even rewarding members for holding tokens through innovative staking and incentive programs.
