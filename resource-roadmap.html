Year 1: Foundation and Early Adoption (Q4 2024 – Q4 2025)

Digital Consult, strategy & growth

Q4 2024: Laying the Groundwork

In the first quarter, we are focused on laying the foundation for MediCureOn. The goal is to ensure that the platform architecture is designed with scalability, security, and integration with blockchain, AI, and IoMT technologies. Key deliverables will include developing the website, launching the ICO, and finalizing our core MVPs. These elements will serve as the foundation for our future growth.

Platform Architecture Design: Finalizing the overall architecture to ensure scalability, security, and the integration of blockchain, AI, and IoMT technologies.

Core MVPs Development:

  • Website Development: Build and host the official MediCureOn website to provide detailed information about the project and the complete ecosystem.
  • ICO Launch (MediCoin Offering): Launching our Initial Coin Offering (ICO) for MediCoin (MCN) to attract investors and raise funds for ecosystem development.
  • Basic Membership Authentication: Enable secure member sign-ups on the platform.
  • Profile Creation: Allow members to create and manage personalized profiles.
  • Registration and Management: Setting up features for event registration and management for members and healthcare providers.
  • AI Training (Initiation): Begin training AI models for healthcare insights and predictive care.

Partnership Outreach: Begin communications with alternative healthcare organizations and schedule meetings with chiropractors, acupuncturists, naturopaths, and others. Establishing these partnerships is essential for expanding the platform in the first year.

By the end of Q4 2024, our goal is to have a fully designed platform architecture, initial MVPs in place, and the groundwork laid for partnerships with alternative healthcare providers.

Q1 2025: Development and Expansion

Building on the progress of Q4, our focus will be on completing the development of our MVPs and onboarding key partners.

  • Complete MVP Development: Finalize user authentication, profile management, AI training, and other core features.
  • Beta Testing: Start a small beta test to gather user feedback and fine-tune the platform.
  • Partnership Agreements: Finalize agreements with a wide range of alternative healthcare providers, including naturopaths, chiropractors, acupuncturists, yoga therapists, homeopaths, Ayurvedic practitioners, and nutritionists.

Q2 2025: Platform Expansion

With MVPs completed, Q2 2025 will focus on expanding the platform to onboard providers and members.

  • Onboard Providers and Members: Scale the platform to onboard more healthcare providers and offer early access to a larger member base.
  • AI and IoMT Integration: Begin integrating real-time health monitoring from IoMT devices, enabling members to receive health insights based on wearable data.

Q3 2025: AI and Data Analytics Rollout

Q3 is dedicated to fully operationalizing AI and MediCoin (MCN) payments.

  • Full AI Capabilities: Launch fully operational AI that offers personalized, predictive healthcare insights for members.
  • Launch MCN Payments: Introduce MediCoin (MCN) as the payment method for healthcare services, establishing our token economy.

Q4 2025: Marketplace and Growth Initiatives

Q4 2025 will see the launch of the MediCureOn marketplace and the start of global expansion.

  • Marketplace Launch: Complete the development of the MediCureOn marketplace. Members will be able to purchase discounted healthcare services from providers using MCN and alternative products. While fiat currencies will be accepted, discounts will only apply when using MCN.
  • Global Expansion: Begin the strategic expansion into underserved markets, focusing on regions where affordable healthcare is most needed. Target areas include Europe, Africa, Asia, and the Middle East.

Year 2: Platform Expansion and Market Penetration

Q1 2026: Onboarding and Integration of Key Healthcare Entities

  • Onboarding Healthcare Providers: Expand our network by integrating additional traditional and alternative healthcare providers. These will include specialized healthcare services such as telemedicine providers, holistic healthcare experts, and institutions specializing in preventive care.
  • Payers & Pharmaceutical Integrations: Collaborate with major health insurance companies and pharmaceutical organizations to establish a seamless connection between payers and providers on the platform.
  • Research Institutes Partnerships: Partner with research institutions to enable innovative healthcare solutions based on MediCureOn’s AI and blockchain-powered platform.

Q2 2026: AI and IoMT First Phase Rollout

  • AI & IoMT-Enabled Monitoring: Launch the first phase of real-time, AI-powered preventive healthcare monitoring, integrating data from IoMT devices (such as wearable health trackers) to provide real-time health data, predictive insights, and personalized recommendations.
  • Partnership with IoMT Manufacturers: Partner with major IoMT device manufacturers to ensure compatibility and seamless data integration with MediCureOn’s platform for predictive care.

Q3 2026: International Expansion and Compliance

  • Global Expansion into Underserved Regions: Focus on expanding into underserved regions, particularly in Africa, Asia, and the Middle East, where affordable healthcare access is limited.
  • Regional Compliance Certifications: Ensure MediCureOn meets all regional compliance requirements, securing necessary certifications such as GDPR (Europe), HIPAA (US), and others based on location.

Q4 2026: AI-Powered Predictive Analytics & Service Automation

  • Advanced AI Analytics Development: Continue the development of AI-powered analytics that deliver predictive healthcare insights, improving the accuracy and timeliness of healthcare interventions.
  • Smart Contracts & Service Automation: Begin automating processes using blockchain-enabled smart contracts, enabling faster, more secure, and transparent healthcare service management.

Year 3: Global Expansion and Advanced Features

Q1 2027: Full Global Rollout & Increased Member Adoption

  • Global Market Rollout: Complete the full rollout of the platform in major global markets, emphasizing underserved and emerging markets.
  • Increased Member Adoption: Focus on increasing member adoption by offering membership promotions, partnerships with local healthcare institutions, and public awareness campaigns.

Q2 2027: Telemedicine & Decentralized Health Management

  • Telemedicine Expansion: Launch an array of telemedicine services, offering members access to remote consultations and virtual healthcare management using MCN.
  • Decentralized Health Services: Introduce decentralized healthcare services where patients can manage their own medical records, and control access to their data using MediCureOn’s blockchain-powered health management tools.

Q3 2027: Private Blockchain & Financial Services

  • Private Blockchain for Medical Records: Begin integrating MediCureOn’s private blockchain for medical records, offering full transparency, security, and patient control over medical data.
  • Investing in Financial Services: Develop MediCureOn’s DeFi offerings, including a healthcare-specific financial exchange and potentially a digital bank, allowing for seamless healthcare payments through MCN.

Q4 2027: Fully Developed Marketplace and Self-Sustaining Ecosystem

  • Marketplace with 3rd Party Integrations: Launch a fully developed healthcare marketplace, integrating third-party vendors, alternative healthcare providers, and various products and services, all powered by MCN.
  • Advanced AI for Personalized Care Plans: Enable advanced AI features for fully personalized care plans, tailored to individual health data and preferences.
  • Self-Sustaining Ecosystem Using MCN: At this stage, MediCureOn will become a self-sustaining ecosystem where MCN is the primary currency, powering all services, partnerships, and user activities.

Quarterly Updates

To ensure transparency, MediCureOn is committed to providing quarterly updates to investors, members, and stakeholders. Each quarter, we will publish comprehensive reports that detail our progress, partnerships, member growth, and ecosystem development.

Future Vision

While our three-year roadmap is ambitious, our vision extends far beyond this initial period. MediCureOn aims to:

  • Continue its global expansion, reaching underserved communities around the world.
  • Become the leading platform for AI-powered, personalized, preventive healthcare.
  • Seamlessly integrate with the IoMT ecosystem, providing patients with proactive, real-time care that allows them to take full control of their health.
  • Leverage MCN to facilitate low-cost, efficient healthcare transactions across borders.

Our long-term goal is to create a decentralized, member-driven healthcare ecosystem where patients—not institutions—control their health, costs are dramatically lowered, and alternative healthcare providers are seamlessly integrated with traditional care.
