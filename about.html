Mission Statement:

Our mission at MediCureOn is to democratize healthcare by leveraging blockchain, AI, and cutting-edge IoMT technologies, empowering patients to take control of their health while ensuring transparency, affordability, and access to a wide range of healthcare providers.

Our Story

MediCureOn was born from a need to address the growing gap in healthcare accessibility, costs, and patient control. Traditional systems have become bloated, inefficient, and overly focused on profit, leaving patients burdened with higher costs and fewer choices.

Our founder, Humayun Khan, saw an opportunity to change this by developing a decentralized healthcare platform that integrates alternative healthcare providers, such as acupuncturists, naturopaths, and more, with traditional healthcare options. MediCureOn ensures that all patients can access personalized care regardless of their location, income, or health history.

From its initial concept to a fully backed Microsoft Startup, MediCureOn has grown into a vision for a healthier, more equitable world.
