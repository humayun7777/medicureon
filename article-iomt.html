AI and IoMT, The Future of Predictive Healthcare with MediCureOn

  • By: Humayun Khan
  • 11 September, 2024

Healthcare is undergoing a profound transformation. MediCureOn is working to change that by leveraging AI to identify potential health risks before they manifest. The platform's capabilities are being built with the goal of shifting healthcare from a focus on acute treatment to a focus on preventive care. This is a monumental change: instead of waiting for a problem to escalate, MediCureOn aims to detect it early, recommend interventions, and ultimately improve the patient's quality of life.
